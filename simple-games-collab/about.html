About Valorant Aim Trainer

This is a simple, web‑based aim training tool inspired by Valorant’s shooting mechanics. Choose from multiple difficulties to warm up your crosshair placement and reflexes before jumping into matches.

Future features:

  • Additional game modes (moving targets, timed drills)
  • Leaderboards and stats tracking
  • Custom crosshair settings
